Matoli

Sacred Fruits of Ganesh Chaturthi

Matoli is a traditional decoration hung during Ganesh Chaturthi, featuring sacred fruits, leaves, and flowers suspended from a wooden frame. Each fruit carries deep spiritual significance, representing prosperity, purity, and devotion to Lord Ganesha.

This ancient tradition symbolizes the abundance of nature and the blessings of the harvest season. The hanging fruits create a beautiful canopy above the Ganesha idol, representing the divine connection between earth's bounty and spiritual devotion.
